1.An Examiner will monitor your discipline during the test.
2.The time allocated to you is 30 minutes.
The examiner is responsible for keeping track of time.
3.You will not be allowed to answer any question once the stipulated time is over.
4.During the test, if you wish to skip the question, you may press 0 key.
Please note that once you skip the question, you will not be allowed
to review or return to that question again.
5.You are required to enter numeric entries of the answer index.
Any inappropriate entry of answer will cause waste of time
and you will be returned to the same question again.
6.During the test, if you wish to quit, press Esc key.
